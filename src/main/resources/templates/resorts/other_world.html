Other World Resorts

There are many ski resorts around the world that are outside of North America, South America, and Europe. These ski resorts are found in places such as Japan, New Zealand, and Australia. Ski resorts in Japan, like Niseko, are known for their incredible snow quality, while ski resorts in New Zealand, like Queenstown, offer a wide range of winter sports and activities. In Australia, ski resorts like Perisher and Thredbo provide a unique skiing experience in the southern hemisphere.
